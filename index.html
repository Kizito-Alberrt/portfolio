About Me

My introduction

One of the most fulfilling aspects of my work has been the opportunity to introduce young people in Mozambique to the transformative power of technology. Through various initiatives and programs, we've provided access to computer classes and tech education, opening doors to a world of knowledge and possibilities. We've witnessed firsthand the excitement and eagerness in the eyes of these young minds as they learn to code, explore digital literacy, and unleash their creativity through technology. Whether it's through coding workshops, robotics clubs, or simply providing access to online resources, we've seen how these experiences have sparked curiosity, ignited passions, and equipped them with valuable skills for the future
